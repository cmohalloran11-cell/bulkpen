How it works. This page is a thin client: a Python backend pulls the schedule, probables, season stats, and game logs from statsapi.mlb.com (via python-mlb-statsapi) and does all the scoring server-side, then hands this page one ready-made payload. Opener risk = heuristic from each starter's season innings-per-appearance, relief share, and whether a non-starter is listed to start. Bulk Score ranks relievers by innings-per-appearance, swingman history, and workload, then adjusts for recent usage (last-7-day innings & days of rest) to flag who's actually available.
Model-free heuristics, not official projections — a smart shortlist, not a guarantee. No affiliation with MLB.
